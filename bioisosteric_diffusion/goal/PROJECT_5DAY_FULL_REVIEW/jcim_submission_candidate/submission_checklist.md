## Manual Items Before ACS Upload

- Add final author names, affiliations, corresponding-author email, funding, acknowledgments, and conflict-of-interest statements if required by the submission system.
- Confirm whether ACS requests a table-of-contents graphic for this manuscript type.
- Confirm whether the large processed candidate matrices will be deposited before publication or described as redistribution-restricted derived artifacts.
- Replace "upon acceptance" DOI language after an archival DOI is minted.

## Claim Boundaries to Preserve

- The 77-feature scorer is post-audit locked, not fully prospective feature selection.
- A4C is a computational triage proxy, not expert validation.
- Structure-derived labels do not establish activity preservation.
- No wet-lab validation or production medicinal-chemistry decision-making is claimed.
