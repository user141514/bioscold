Figure 5. Dual-mode provenance triage for exploratory replacement proposals. Conservative proposals follow the HGB route, whereas exploratory proposals follow the Borda(DE,HGB) route and are stratified by provenance. G4 denotes HGB/Borda consensus, G3 denotes DE-elevated replacements supported by Borda but not HGB, and G2 denotes Borda-emergent replacements outside HGB and DE support. A4C is used only as a computational triage proxy; coverage limitations, especially for G4, prevent interpretation as experimental validation.
