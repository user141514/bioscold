Figure 3. Secondary blind Top-10 performance across base rankers, fusion baselines, and candidate-level scorers. Points show query-level Top-10 accuracy on 13,347 secondary blind queries; error bars denote 95% bootstrap confidence intervals. The initial 82-feature scorer is the prospective candidate-level blind result. The 77-feature scorer is a locked post-audit feature-pruned result after removal of prior_ranks. Best-of-DE+HGB is shown as a diagnostic bound for per-query selection between DE and HGB, not as a performance limit for candidate-level models.
